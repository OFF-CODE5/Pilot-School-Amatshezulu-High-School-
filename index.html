About Our School

Located in the heart of Hammarsdale, Pinetown, KwaZulu-Natal

AmatsheZulu High School is a public secondary school dedicated to providing quality education in a supportive and inclusive environment. We are committed to fostering comprehensive curriculum development that equips students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in all aspects of life.

As a No-Fee-paying school with a quintile rating of 3, we strive to meet the educational needs of our community while adhering to government guidelines for basic education. Our institution is recognized for nurturing a supportive and inclusive learning atmosphere.

Operating under the KwaZulu-Natal Education Department with NatEmis number 500102860, we are an institution committed to providing the highest standard of education.
